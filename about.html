About Angkorism

Your guide to Cambodia's ancient wonders, timeless culture, and golden horizons.

Our Story

Born from a love of
Khmer heritage

Angkorism began as a personal journey — two students from Siem Reap who grew up in the shadow of the world's greatest temple complex and wanted to share its magic with the rest of the world.

What started as a photography blog in 2022 has grown into a full travel guide covering temples, nature, city life, and centuries-old traditions across Cambodia. Every photo, every word, and every recommendation comes from people who truly call this place home.

We believe travel is most meaningful when it is rooted in respect — for the land, the people, and the stories etched into every stone.

What we stand for

Our values

Respectful Tourism

We advocate for responsible, low-impact travel that preserves Angkor's sites for future generations and respects local communities.

Authentic Storytelling

Every article and photograph is created on the ground by Cambodians. No stock photos, no recycled narratives — only lived experience.

Community First

We spotlight local guides, family-run guesthouses, and small businesses — because the best travel experiences begin with people, not booking platforms.

Historical Accuracy

Khmer history deserves precision. We cross-reference every historical claim with academic sources and consult local scholars before publishing.
